Our Programs

Since its inception the Foundation has dedicated 100% of all funds raised directly to programs that benefit our youth, primarily our at-risk youth.

Junior CSI Academy

Call (209) 456-8155 for details.

Chief's Foundation Academy

Comprehensive leadership and citizenship training program for youth.

Chamber of Commerce Youth Leadership Project

Partnership program developing future community leaders.

Second Harvest Food Bank Food for Thought, Bread of Life

Addressing food insecurity among local youth and families.

Homeless Children's Christmas Shopping

Providing holiday support for children experiencing homelessness.

Manteca, East Union, and Sierra High School's Sober Graduation

Supporting safe graduation celebrations for all local high schools.

Agape Foster Family Sponsor

Supporting foster families in our community.

Little League Sponsor

Promoting youth athletics and teamwork through baseball.

MUSD Careers in Law Enforcement Educational Scholarship

Supporting students pursuing careers in law enforcement.

Manteca Senior Center Sponsor

Supporting our senior community members.

Anti-Gang Youth Rally Program

Community events promoting positive choices and gang prevention.

Manteca Boys and Girls Club Sponsorship

Supporting safe after-school and summer programs for youth.

Project Blue: Healthy Rooms and 10K with a Cop

Health and wellness initiatives connecting police with community.

Child Abuse Prevention Council

Supporting efforts to protect children from abuse and neglect.

Agriculture in Motion Science Camp

Educational programs connecting youth with agricultural sciences.

Shop with a Cop

Building positive relationships between police and youth through community shopping events.
