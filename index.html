Our Mission

A choice is a powerful thing. To realize that you have a say in the direction your life takes and that there are others out there who will help you when you make the right choices.

100%

of all funds raised go directly to programs that benefit at-risk youth

The Manteca Police Chief's Foundation's purpose is to expose the youth of our community to positive choices and goals to aspire to and to help them achieve them.

Our Programs

Mentorship & Leadership

Connecting young people with positive role models who guide their personal growth and develop essential leadership skills.

Vocational Training

Providing practical job skills and career preparation to help young people build successful futures.

Recreation Programs

Engaging activities that promote teamwork, healthy competition, and positive social interaction.

Gang Prevention

Prevention and intervention programs designed to keep youth away from gang involvement and toward positive choices.
